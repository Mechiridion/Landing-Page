How It Works

1

Connect Scanner

Plug the Mechiridion OBD Scanner into your vehicle's OBD-II port, usually located under the dashboard near the steering column.

2

Download INFOCAR

Install the INFOCAR app on your iOS or Android device from the respective app store.

3
